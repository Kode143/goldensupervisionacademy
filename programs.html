Our Programs

Montessori Program

Our Montessori program provides a safe and nurturing environment where children can learn and grow at their own pace. With a focus on hands-on learning and exploration, our Montessori program helps children develop a love for learning that will last a lifetime.

Primary Program

Our Primary program provides a strong foundation in literacy, numeracy, and social skills. With a focus on inquiry-based learning and experiential activities, our Primary program prepares students for success in their future academic endeavors.

Secondary Program

Our Secondary program provides students with a comprehensive education that prepares them for success in their post-secondary studies and future careers. With a wide range of academic and elective courses, our Secondary program offers something for every student.

Career Counselling Program

Our Career Counselling program provides students with the guidance and resources they need to explore their career interests and make informed decisions about their future. With individualized support and access to a wide range of resources, our Career Counselling program helps students achieve their goals.

Computer Courses

Our Computer Courses program offers students the opportunity to develop essential computer skills that are essential in today's digital age. With a focus on hands-on learning and real-world applications, our Computer Courses program prepares students for success in their future academic and career endeavors.

About Us

Golden Supervision Academy is a school located in Chapakot-08, Syagja, Nepal. We offer a wide range of courses and facilities to our students, including a well-equipped library, modern classrooms, and state-of-the-art sports facilities. Our goal is to provide a high-quality education to all of our students and help them achieve their full potential.
