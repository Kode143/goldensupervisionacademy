Computer Lab

Our computer lab is equipped with state-of-the-art computers and software to give our students the best learning experience. Our students learn various programming languages, graphics design, and software applications to help them prepare for the ever-changing technology industry.

Games and Sports

We believe that physical activity is an essential part of a student's overall development. Our students have access to a wide range of sports facilities, including football, basketball, badminton, and more. We also offer art classes to help our students express themselves creatively.

scholarship

Our school provides scholarships based on students' performance to encourage and reward academic excellence. We believe that all students should have equal access to quality education, and we strive to provide financial assistance to those who demonstrate outstanding academic achievement. Our scholarship program is designed to motivate students to work hard and excel in their studies by recognizing their efforts and accomplishments. Through this program, we hope to inspire our students to reach their full potential and pursue their academic and professional goals with confidence and determination. We are committed to supporting our students' academic success and ensuring that every student has the opportunity to achieve their dreams.

School Bus

At our school, we understand that many of our students live in remote areas where public transportation is scarce or nonexistent. To ensure that all our students have access to quality education, we provide a school bus service that picks them up from their homes and drops them off at school. Many of our students have to travel more than an hour on foot, through dense forests, rugged terrain, or unsafe neighborhoods to get to school. By providing a reliable and safe transportation service, we not only save our students from the physical exertion and dangers of walking long distances but also enable them to reach school on time and be prepared for their classes. Our buses are driven by experienced and trained drivers who follow all safety protocols and ensure the well-being of our students. We are committed to providing our students with a safe, comfortable, and efficient transportation service that enables them to pursue their academic goals and dreams.

Science Lab

Our science lab is equipped with modern equipment and facilities to help our students explore and experiment with various scientific concepts. Our students learn through hands-on experimentation and develop a deep understanding of various scientific principles.

Montessori

Our school provides Montessori facilities for young children to foster their natural curiosity and love for learning. With Montessori education, our school aims to provide a nurturing environment where children can develop their cognitive, emotional, and social skills at their own pace. The Montessori approach emphasizes hands-on learning, self-directed activity, and collaborative play.

Peaceful Environment

Step into our school and experience a serene and supportive environment that nurtures your child's academic and personal growth. Our school is carefully designed to provide a peaceful and safe haven for our students to learn and explore. With lush greenery and gardens surrounding the school, your child can enjoy a refreshing and calming atmosphere that promotes focus and creativity. Our modern facilities, including well-equipped classrooms, state-of-the-art libraries, healthy cafeterias, and clean drinking water stations, ensure that your child's academic and health needs are fully met. We believe that a supportive and engaging learning environment is crucial for your child's success, and our school's holistic approach to education guarantees that your child's intellectual, social, and emotional development is nurtured throughout their time with us.

Physical Traning and Morning Prayer

At our school, we believe that physical fitness and spiritual well-being are essential for a student's overall development. That's why we offer daily physical training sessions and morning prayers to all our students. These activities help students to start their day with a positive and focused mindset, and improve their physical fitness, coordination, and agility. Through regular physical exercise, our students develop healthy habits and a sense of discipline that can help them in all aspects of their lives. Similarly, morning prayers provide students with an opportunity to connect with their inner selves, express gratitude, and seek blessings for the day ahead.

About Us
